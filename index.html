My story

My IT story has been rather unorthodox. I studied design and music theory in college. Then I had a career working as a bench jeweler spanning over 10 years. I transitioned to IT by working as a web-master for a few years, learning a little bit of CSS, HTML, JS and Liquid Template Language on the job, where my duties were making sure that web-site was up and running smoothly and up-to-date 24/7.

Finally, I went to a bootcamp where I had an opportunity to study a modern front-end tech stack in detail and got proficient in Native JS, Bootstrap, React, Material UI and more.
